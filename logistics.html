Ocean Freight

Ocean Freights are used for the transportation of large loads of goods by sea. One of the world’s biggest modes of transportation for carrying heavy goods, choosing the right ocean freight service provider can work wonders.

For cargoes of 13 cubic metres or less are ideal for shipment through LCL. LCL is the cheapest mode of transportation for importing smaller volume cargo. For importers, LCL can prove to be useful as they can purchase smaller quantities of materials needed for their inventory.

If you have shipments weighing more than 15 cubic meters, our FCL services provide an economical solution. With faster transit times and less handling time required at the origin and destination, FCL is a reliable and efficient option. Once the container is unloaded from the vessel, your FCL shipment is picked up from the terminal and delivered directly to the designated facility.

Air Freight

Air freight is the fastest mode of transportation for moving goods, making it an ideal choice for urgent shipments. With air freight services, you can significantly reduce transit times compared to other modes of transport such as sea or land. This speed is especially beneficial when transporting high-value small goods or time-sensitive items that require quick delivery.
Air freight services, like the ones offered by Plutus, provide real-time visibility of your shipments from pick-up to delivery. Dedicated teams actively monitor the progress of your goods and ensure they reach their destination on time. This level of visibility allows you to stay updated on the status of your shipment, providing peace of mind and the ability to plan accordingly.
Air freight is not only fast but also dependable for carrying heavy goods. With dependable arrival and departure times, air freight ensures the timely transportation of heavy cargo.

Inland Transportation

Ground freight services, offered by Plutus, provide an efficient and cost-effective solution for shipping goods. This mode of transport is particularly advantageous for heavy, large, or hazardous materials that may not be suitable for air or sea transportation. Ground shipping allows for easy distribution of goods within a specific region or country, making it a practical choice for domestic or cross-border shipments.
Whether it’s cross-country or within your state, ground transportation networks are well-established and can efficiently navigate roads, highways, and infrastructure within the specified region. This level of accessibility and flexibility allows for timely delivery of goods, ensuring customer satisfaction and meeting supply chain demands.
Ground shipping is commonly utilized by international flight shippers as a complementary mode of transport for the movement of goods. While air freight offers speed and efficiency for long distances, ground transportation provides the necessary connectivity between airports, cargo terminals, and final delivery points.
